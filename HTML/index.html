ABOUT US

At KVDST, we are dedicated to discovering and nurturing musical talent from across Canada.

Our mission is to provide a platform for emerging and local artists, giving them the support and resources they need to thrive in the music industry.

Join us on this journey as we bring you the best in music, from indie pop and electronic beats to funk, soul, and Americana. We believe in the power of music to connect people and create unforgettable experiences.

"Aurora" by Stellar Dreams.

  • Review: Stellar Dreams’ "Aurora" is a mesmerizing journey through dreamy synth-pop landscapes. The album opens with the ethereal track "Northern Lights," setting the tone for a collection of songs that blend lush electronic textures with heartfelt lyrics. Each track on "Aurora" is a carefully crafted masterpiece.
  • Standout Tracks: "Celestial Embrace," "Galactic Pulse"

"Wanderlust" by The Urban Nomads.

  • Review: The Urban Nomads make a strong impression with their debut album "Wanderlust." This indie folk album is rich with storytelling and acoustic melodies that transport listeners to different places and times. The band’s harmonious vocals and intricate guitar work are evident in tracks like "Roaming Free" and "Sunset Horizons."
  • Standout Tracks: "Homeward Bound," "Trail of Stars"

Read Review

"Echoes" by Lunar Echo.

  • Review: Lunar Echo’s "Echoes" is a hidden gem in the ambient music genre. The album is an immersive experience, with soundscapes that are both soothing and thought-provoking. Tracks like "Silent Waves" and "Midnight Reflections" are perfect examples of the duo’s ability to create music that resonates on a deeper level.
  • Standout Tracks: "Whispering Pines," "Dreamscape"
