Luna Echo

Background: Emerging from the indie music scene in Vancouver, British Columbia, Luna Echo has been captivating audiences with their haunting vocals and dreamy soundscapes. Their journey began when they self-released their debut EP, "Eclipse," in 2018. Music Style: Known for their ethereal mix of indie pop and electronic elements, Luna Echo's music evokes a sense of nostalgia and wonder. Achievements: They have gained a loyal following through performances at local venues and festivals. Their single "Moonlit Dreams" has been featured on several indie music blogs. Fun Fact: Luna Echo often incorporates visual art into their live performances, creating a multisensory experience for their audience..

The Velvet Vibes

Background: Based in Toronto, Ontario, The Velvet Vibes is a dynamic band known for their fusion of funk, soul, and rock. Formed in 2017, the band quickly gained a reputation for their high-energy live shows. Music Style: Their sound is a blend of groovy basslines, soulful vocals, and electrifying guitar riffs, reminiscent of classic funk and modern rock. Achievements: The Velvet Vibes have performed at numerous local festivals and have opened for several well-known acts in the area. Their debut album "Groove Machine" received positive reviews from local music critics. Fun Fact: The band members met at a local jam session and instantly connected over their shared love of funk and soul music.

Nova Cascade

Background: Hailing from Montreal, Quebec, Nova Cascade is an up-and-coming electronic music producer known for their innovative sound design and infectious beats. They began producing music in their bedroom studio in 2019. Music Style: Combining elements of house, techno, and ambient music, Nova Cascade creates immersive soundscapes that transport listeners to another world. Achievements: Nova Cascade has released several singles and remixes that have gained traction on streaming platforms. Their track "Galactic Voyage" was featured in a popular electronic music playlist. Fun Fact: Nova Cascade often draws inspiration from science fiction and space exploration, which is reflected in their music and visuals.

The Midnight Ramblers

Background: Originating from Calgary, Alberta, The Midnight Ramblers is a local band that brings a fresh twist to Americana and folk music. Formed in 2016, the band has built a strong local following. Music Style: Their sound is characterized by rich harmonies, acoustic instrumentation, and heartfelt storytelling, capturing the essence of Canadian roots music. Achievements: The Midnight Ramblers have released two EPs and have been featured on local radio stations. They regularly play at popular venues in Calgary and have a dedicated fan base. Fun Fact: The band's name was inspired by their habit of writing and rehearsing late into the night.

ABOUT US

At KVDST, we are dedicated to discovering and nurturing musical talent from across Canada.

Our mission is to provide a platform for emerging and local artists, giving them the support and resources they need to thrive in the music industry.

Join us on this journey as we bring you the best in music, from indie pop and electronic beats to funk, soul, and Americana. We believe in the power of music to connect people and create unforgettable experiences.
