Sound, Strategy, Signal

Artist Discovery

We look for artists with a world around their sound: voice, visuals, story, and a live identity that can grow beyond one release cycle.

Release Architecture

Every single, EP, visual, and announcement is built as part of a clear campaign so momentum feels intentional from first teaser to final show.

Live Culture

From intimate rooms to festival stages, KVDST designs nights that make discovery feel personal, cinematic, and worth remembering.

Visual Worlds

Artwork, merch, video, photography, and stage direction are treated as extensions of the music, not decoration after the fact.

ABOUT US

KVDST is a Vancouver-rooted label for atmospheric artists with a sharp sense of place.

We build campaigns for music that feels cinematic, intimate, and restless. Our roster moves through indie pop, electronic texture, soul, rock, and folk, but the common thread is emotional detail.

The label works hands-on with artists across recordings, visual direction, shows, merch, press, and fan community so each project arrives as a complete world.

OUR VISION

Build a label where emerging Canadian artists can make ambitious work without losing the strangeness that made the work matter.

OUR MISSION

Pair artist development with release strategy, visual language, community, and live performance so every project can travel farther.

OUR STRENGTHS

A close ear for mood, a strong visual identity, and a Vancouver perspective that gives the label a distinctive nocturnal edge.
